The IMDL @ KSU is aimed at making transportation safer through wireless communications and computer networking. 
Our aim is to explore and improve [Intelligent Transportation Systems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intelligent_transportation_system) through experimental research and software development to develop new sensing and mobile computing approaches using machine learning / artificial intelligence throughout the network fabric.
